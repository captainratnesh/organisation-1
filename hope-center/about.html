Introduction

Atharv Welfare Society, Established in 2010, aspires to work towards creating a better world which provides basic health, education and empowerment to every citizen. It also ventures with Corporate to transform the lives of people by enabling them to hold the power to create opportunity for themselves and others. To implement sustainable programs that improve access nationwide to investment, opportunity, and lifesaving services now and for future generations

Mission

Atharv Welfare Society started its mission to spread the Importance of sanitation/ Health/ Education on a large scale with the view that facilities must reach as many people as possible and as quickly as possible. Thus Atharv Welfare Society was established as a Public Charitable Trust by Mr. Ghanshyam Tiwari & a team of likeminded people. Today, it is one among the largest growing NGO working in the Indian Health/ Education sector. Our main motto is to empower underprivileged people of India through relevant education, innovative healthcare and market-focused livelihood programs. Atharv Welfare Society is to deploy best possible methodology and technology for achieving ideal SROI (social return on investment), to practice and promote good governance. To link business competitiveness of the corporate with social development initiatives; also to sensitize privileged children, youth and citizens in general to promote Civic Driven Change.
